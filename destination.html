EV ENABLEMENT IS A POSITIVE SUM GAME

Customer Experience: Your guests who drive an electric car will be delighted to visit those properties where they can plug-in their new EV as they have a good time.

Fleet Electrification: Captive charging facility can be the first step towards moving your fleet to electric mobility. The savings on cost and carbon credits has already made electric mobility the most compelling choice for fleet operators.

Compliance with Green Building Certification: While EV Charging facility earns crucial points in green-building certification, it helps in improving operational efficiency as well. EV enablement of your premises will add value to both aspects of Quality.

Clean Energy for for Green Mobility: If the premise is already solar powered, you are at an advantage to claim the badge of EV enablement tending to a net-zero carbon footprint.
